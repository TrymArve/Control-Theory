Consider:
$$
\begin{align}
& \Sigma_1 \quad : \quad \dot{x}_1 = f_1(x_1,x_2) \\
& \Sigma_2 \quad : \quad \dot{x}_2 = f_2(x_2)
\end{align}
$$
