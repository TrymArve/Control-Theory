A [[Set|set]] $\mathbb{S}$, of a [[Metric Space|metric space]] $(\mathbb{S},d)$, is *closed* iff:
- $x\in \mathbb{S} \quad \forall x: x$ is the limit of a [[Cauchy Sequence|Cauchy sequence]] in $\mathbb{S}$
