**Definition:**
A *commutative group (Abelian group)* is a [[02 -Group|group]]; $(\mathbb{S},\oplus)$, where the [[Binary Operation|operation]] $\oplus$ is [[Commutativity|commutative]]. That is;
- $a\oplus b = b\oplus a \quad \forall a,b \in \mathbb{S}$
